# SkillPay Pitch Deck Outline (Level 5 Blue Belt)

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title:** SkillPay
- **Subtitle:** Learn. Prove it. Get paid.
- **Visuals:** SkillPay logo and a vibrant mockup of the dashboard.
- **Talking Point:** "Welcome to SkillPay, the first truly decentralized platform where mentors escrow funds for real-world tasks, and learners get paid instantly upon proving their skills."

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Bullet Points:**
  - Traditional education is disconnected from earning.
  - Freelance platforms have high fees and trust issues.
  - Proof of skill is often theoretical, not practical.
- **Talking Point:** "Learners spend months acquiring skills without a clear path to monetization, while mentors struggle to find verified talent. The friction of traditional payments and trust makes it harder for both parties."

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **Bullet Points:**
  - **Learn & Earn:** Mentors post real challenges with an XLM bounty.
  - **Proof of Work:** Learners submit GitHub repos and live links.
  - **Instant Settlement:** Built on Stellar, payouts happen instantly and securely via Freighter wallet.
- **Talking Point:** "SkillPay solves this by escrowing funds directly on the Stellar blockchain. When a mentor approves a submission, the learner is paid instantly. No middlemen, no waiting periods."

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity
- **Bullet Points:**
  - **EdTech & Web3:** Merging the $340B EdTech market with the gig economy.
  - **Target Audience:** Bootcamps, DAOs, Open Source projects, and independent mentors.
- **Talking Point:** "The intersection of Web3 and education is mostly untapped. We are targeting DAOs looking to bounty out tasks and educational platforms wanting to incentivize learning."

## Slide 5: Product Architecture (Tech Stack)
- **Bullet Points:**
  - **Frontend:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS, React Joyride (UX)
  - **Backend:** Node.js, Express, MongoDB Atlas
  - **Blockchain:** Stellar SDK, Freighter API for seamless client-side signing.
- **Talking Point:** "We built a robust Web2.5 experience. The app feels like a modern SaaS product but leverages Stellar's low fees and high speed for the financial layer."

## Slide 6: Growth & Traction (Level 5 Validation)
- **Bullet Points:**
  - **Users:** Onboarded 50+ testnet users.
  - **Feedback Loop:** Implemented UI/UX changes (Notifications, Analytics, Onboarding Tours) based directly on user interviews.
- **Talking Point:** "We didn't just build in the dark. We onboarded over 50 testnet users, collected feedback via Google Forms, and iterated the product to improve retention and onboarding."

## Slide 7: Future Roadmap
- **Bullet Points:**
  - **Phase 1 (Now):** MVP on Testnet, Escrow functionality.
  - **Phase 2:** Mainnet launch, Soroban smart contracts for automated dispute resolution.
  - **Phase 3:** Integration with major coding bootcamps as a 'Bounty' layer.
- **Talking Point:** "Our next step is Mainnet. We plan to introduce Soroban smart contracts to fully decentralize the escrow and dispute resolution processes."

- **Bullet Points:**

  - Scan QR code to try the live demo!
- **Talking Point:** "Thank you! Check out our live demo and join us in revolutionizing how people learn and earn."
