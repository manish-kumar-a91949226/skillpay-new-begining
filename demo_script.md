# SkillPay Demo Video Script (Level 5)

**Length:** ~3-5 Minutes
**Prep:** Have your Freighter Wallet open, logged in, and set to Testnet. Have two accounts ready (Mentor and Learner).

---

### [0:00 - 0:30] Introduction & Problem
**Screen:** Show the SkillPay Landing Page.
**Script:**
> "Hi everyone, this is SkillPay—a decentralized platform built on Stellar that connects mentors who need work done with learners who want to earn while they learn. Traditional freelance platforms have high fees, and traditional education lacks direct monetization. SkillPay solves this by escrowing funds on-chain and paying learners instantly when their proof-of-work is approved."

### [0:30 - 1:15] The Mentor Flow (Posting a Challenge)
**Screen:** Log in as a Mentor. Show the new 'Interactive Onboarding Tour' popping up.
**Script:**
> "Let's log in as a Mentor. Notice our new interactive onboarding tour—a feature we added based on direct user feedback to help new users navigate the platform. I'll skip it for now."
> "I'm going to post a new challenge for 50 XLM. When I click 'Fund & Post', SkillPay integrates directly with my Freighter wallet. I sign the transaction client-side—meaning the platform never holds my private keys."

### [1:15 - 2:00] The Learner Flow (Discover & Submit)
**Screen:** Switch to a Learner account. Show the Dashboard and Notifications.
**Script:**
> "Now I'm logged in as a Learner. In my Dashboard, you can see the new Visual Analytics charts we built using Recharts—another feature requested by our beta testers to track earnings growth."
> "I also have a notification in my new Notification Center that a new challenge was posted! Let's go to the challenge, submit my GitHub repo link, and hit 'Submit Work'."

### [2:00 - 2:45] Approval & Instant Payout
**Screen:** Switch back to the Mentor account. Approve the submission.
**Script:**
> "Back as the Mentor, I can review the learner's code. It looks great, so I click 'Approve'. Behind the scenes, the backend signs the transaction releasing the escrowed funds directly to the learner's wallet on the Stellar network. No manual wire transfers, no waiting 3 days."

### [2:45 - 3:15] Traction & User Growth
**Screen:** Show the `user_feedback_responses.csv` or the Google Form spreadsheet.
**Script:**
> "For this milestone, we focused heavily on growth. We successfully onboarded over 50 testnet users. We collected detailed feedback via Google Forms, which directly drove our latest UX updates: the analytics dashboard, the onboarding tour, and the notification center."

### [3:15 - 3:30] Conclusion
**Screen:** Landing page / GitHub Repo.
**Script:**
> "SkillPay is ready to scale. By combining the speed of Stellar with the massive EdTech market, we are redefining proof-of-skill. Thanks for watching!"
